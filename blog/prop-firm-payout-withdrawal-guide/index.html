How Long Does It Take to Get Paid from a Prop Firm?

Total Time = Processing (1-3 days) + Delivery (0-5 days) = 1-8 days end-to-end

Understanding Payout Frequencies

Payout frequency determines when you can request withdrawals, separate from how long processing takes.

1. Daily Payouts

TopStep, Tradeify

How it works: Request a payout any business day, and it's processed the next business day. Meet profit criteria (typically $50-$100 minimum), submit request by cutoff time (usually 4 PM ET), receive funds 24-48 hours later.

Pros: Fastest access to profits, maximum flexibility, great for traders needing frequent cash flow

Cons: May have minimum profit requirements or consistency rules (TopStep doesn't allow withdrawals after 3 losing days)

2. On-Demand Payouts

Apex, My Funded Futures, FunderPro

How it works: Request withdrawal whenever you want, no scheduled cycles. Must meet minimum ($50-$100), and processing begins immediately. Receive funds in 1-3 business days depending on payment method.

Pros: Complete flexibility, no waiting for payout cycles, withdraw after every profitable day if desired

Cons: Still requires 1-3 day processing, may have minimum holding periods (5-30 days after funding)

3. Bi-Weekly Payouts

FTMO, FundedNext, Funding Traders

How it works: Scheduled payouts every 14 days (e.g., 1st and 15th of month). Submit request during cycle, receive payment on payout date + processing time (1-3 days).

Pros: Predictable schedule, encourages accumulating larger payouts (saving on fees), structured approach

Cons: Must wait up to 14 days even if profits are ready, less flexible than daily/on-demand

4. Weekly Payouts

Various Mid-Tier Firms

How it works: Payouts processed every 7 days (typically Friday or Monday). Submit by Wednesday for Friday processing.

Pros: More frequent than bi-weekly, still structured enough to accumulate meaningful amounts

Cons: Less common, still requires waiting up to 7 days

5. Monthly Payouts

Some Budget Firms

How it works: Payouts once per month on a specific date. Least flexible option.

Pros: Accumulates larger amounts, minimizes transaction fees

Cons: Poor cash flow management, can wait 30+ days, red flag if this is the ONLY option (may indicate cashflow issues)

The Complete Withdrawal Process: Step-by-Step

Here's exactly what happens when you request a payout from a prop firm:

1 Meet Minimum Requirements

Ensure you've met: Minimum payout amount ($50-$100), Minimum trading days (5-10 days), Minimum funded period (some firms require 30 days), Account in good standing (no rule violations), KYC verification completed

2 Submit Withdrawal Request

Log into your trader dashboard, navigate to "Payouts" or "Withdrawals" section, enter amount you want to withdraw (leave enough to maintain minimum balance if required), select payment method, confirm banking/payment details are correct

3 Firm Review and Approval

Automated Approval (Instant-24 hours): Most established firms use automated systems checking: No open trades, Account balance sufficient, No recent rule violations, KYC completed. Manual Review (1-3 days): First payout, Large amounts ($5K+), Account flagged for review, Documentation issues

4 Payment Processing

Once approved, firm initiates payment through chosen method: Crypto: 0-24 hours, PayPal: 1-3 days, Wire: 2-5 days, Check: 5-10 days

5 Receive Funds

Funds appear in your account. Check confirmation email, verify amount matches request, convert crypto to fiat if needed, report for tax purposes

KYC Verification Requirements

All legitimate prop firms require Know Your Customer (KYC) verification before your first payout. This is mandated by financial regulations and anti-money laundering laws.

Standard KYC Documents Required:

  • Government-Issued Photo ID: Passport, driver's license, or national ID card (must be valid/not expired)
  • Proof of Address (within 90 days): Utility bill (electric, gas, water), bank statement, government correspondence, rental agreement
  • Selfie Verification: Photo of you holding your ID next to your face (prevents identity theft)
  • Payment Method Verification: Bank statement showing account number for wire transfers, Crypto wallet address screenshot, PayPal account email confirmation
  • Tax Forms (for US traders or large amounts): W-9 form (US citizens), W-8BEN form (non-US citizens), Tax ID or SSN

KYC Processing Times:

  • Automated Systems: 1-4 hours during business hours
  • Manual Review: 24-72 hours
  • Issues/Resubmission: 3-7 days

Common Payout Delays and How to Avoid Them

1. Incomplete KYC Verification

Cause: Missing documents, expired ID, address proof older than 90 days

Solution: Complete KYC immediately after funding, use recent utility bills, ensure all documents are clear and legible

Delay Time: 3-7 additional days

2. Open Trades During Withdrawal Request

Cause: Some firms require all positions closed before processing payouts

Solution: Close all trades before submitting withdrawal request, check firm-specific requirements

Delay Time: 1-2 days until trades are closed

3. Incorrect Banking Information

Cause: Wrong account number, invalid SWIFT code, crypto wallet address typo

Solution: Double-check all payment details, use copy-paste for crypto addresses (never type manually), verify with small test transaction if available

Delay Time: 5-10 days for correction and reprocessing

4. Requesting Payout Too Soon

Cause: Some firms require 5-30 day waiting period after funding before first withdrawal

Solution: Read payout terms before joining, plan for minimum funded period

Delay Time: Up to 30 days

5. Weekend/Holiday Requests

Cause: Requests submitted Friday evening or during holidays sit until next business day

Solution: Submit requests Monday-Thursday morning for fastest processing

Delay Time: 2-3 extra days over weekends

Total time = Firm processing (1-3 days) + Payment method delivery (0-5 days).

The fastest payout prop firms are: (1) FundedNext - 24-hour processing from payout request, offers crypto (USDT), PayPal, wire transfer; (2) My Funded Futures - Instant approval in most cases, manual review takes 6-12 hours on weekdays if needed; (3) Tradeify - Daily processing, payments issued within 24 hours of approval; (4) FunderPro - 8-hour average processing time; (5) TopStep - 24-48 hour processing, daily payout frequency. What are prop firm withdrawal requirements?

Standard prop firm withdrawal requirements include: (1) KYC Verification - Must complete Know Your Customer verification with government-issued ID, proof of address (utility bill, bank statement within 90 days), sometimes selfie holding ID; processing takes 24-72 hours. (2) Minimum Payout Amount - Typically $50-$100 minimum (FTMO has no minimum, TopStep $50, Apex $50, FundedNext $100). (3) Minimum Trading Period - Some firms require 5-10 trading days or 30 days funded before first withdrawal. (4) Account Compliance - Must not have violated any rules, no open positions during payout request at some firms, account in good standing. (5) Consistency Requirements - Some firms (TopStep) don't allow withdrawals if last 3 days were losses, others have no such restriction. (6) Documentation - May need to provide tax forms (W-9 for US, W-8BEN for international), bank account verification, crypto wallet address. First payout typically takes longer (3-7 days including KYC) than subsequent payouts (1-3 days).

Can I withdraw profits anytime from a prop firm?

Withdrawal timing depends on the firm's payout policy: (1) On-Demand Payouts - Firms like Apex Trader Funding and Take Profit Trader allow withdrawals anytime you want, processed in 1-2 business days with no scheduled cycles. (2) Daily Payouts - TopStep and Tradeify process payouts daily after meeting profit criteria, request by cutoff time and receive next business day. (3) Bi-Weekly Payouts - FTMO processes every 14 days on scheduled dates, must wait for next payout cycle even if profits are ready. (4) Weekly Payouts - Some firms offer weekly processing (every Friday or Monday). (5) Restricted Timing - Budget firms may only allow monthly withdrawals. Best for flexibility: Apex (request anytime) or TopStep (daily processing). Most restrictive: Monthly payout cycles. Important: Even on-demand firms require 1-3 business days for processing and payment delivery, so 'anytime' means you can REQUEST anytime, not receive instantly.
