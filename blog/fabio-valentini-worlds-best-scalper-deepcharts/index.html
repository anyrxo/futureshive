Fabio's Trading Methodology: Market Auction Theory

Fabio's edge comes from his deep understanding of Market Auction Theory‚Äîa framework for understanding how markets facilitate price discovery through auction processes.

Core Principles of Market Auction Theory

1. Value Areas

Markets trade within value areas where buyers and sellers agree on fair value:

  • Price spends most time in value areas (70% rule)
  • Moves outside value are often quickly rejected
  • Value area shifts indicate changing market sentiment

2. Auction Rotations

Markets rotate between:

  • Responsive Trading: Price returns to value after extremes
  • Initiative Trading: New buyers/sellers push price to new value areas
  • Understanding which phase market is in determines trade direction

3. Order Flow & Volume

Fabio analyzes:

  • Buying vs Selling Pressure: Imbalances signal potential moves
  • Volume at Price: Where institutional orders are absorbing flow
  • Delta: Net buying/selling in real-time
  • Absorption: Large orders preventing price movement
